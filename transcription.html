Transcription

The process of copying DNA into mRNA in the nucleus.

Step 1: Transcription

In transcription, a segment of DNA is used to create messenger RNA (mRNA). This process happens inside the nucleus of the cell. The mRNA will later be used as a template for building proteins during translation.
